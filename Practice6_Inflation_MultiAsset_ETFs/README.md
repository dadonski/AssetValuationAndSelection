# Practice 6 — Inflation & Multi-Asset ETFs

Analysis of how inflation (CPI) affects a multi-asset portfolio composed of stock, sovereign bond, and commodity ETFs, plus Bitcoin, using over 10 years of daily/monthly data (17 July 2014 – 12 September 2024).
